Introduction

My name is Carlos, also called Graffe on Twitch. I was born in the Netherlands, and I started with streaming around August 2017. The reason behind streaming was simple for me, it was due to my Youtube channel being watched less slowly.

I really enjoy playing games which led to eventually gathering the courage to broadcast my face on a huge platform, called Twitch. I gathered a stable base of viewers and people that enjoyed the content I streamed into the huge virtual world, this was a point where I felt like I could actually make this part of my life.

Hence I am now a semi-full time streamer trying to gather a following in order to spread funny and enjoyable content!
